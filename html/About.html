    • About the Artist

    Jordan LeGrange is a digital illustrator+graphic designer. Originally a painter, she has transitioned from canvas to screen. She is inspired by all things that flourish in color which is then reflected in her work. She specializes in patterns, illustrative portraits, and a variety of doodles that are often inspired by pop culture. Coffee Addict, die hard fan of the office, and cat mom, Jordan loves expressing her nerdy side in her art work.
